Nutrition Guidelines
Hydration

Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily, more during intense workouts.

Meal Timing

Eat smaller, frequent meals throughout the day to maintain energy levels.

Portion Control

Use proper portion sizes and listen to your body's hunger cues.

Pre-Workout

Eat a light snack 30-60 minutes before exercise for energy.

Post-Workout

Consume protein and carbs within 30 minutes after exercise for recovery.

Whole Foods
